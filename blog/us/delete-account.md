# Delete Account - Mkeka Leo Mobile App

**Effective Date:** May 29, 2026  
**Last Updated:** May 29, 2026

This page explains how users of the **Mkeka Leo mobile app** can delete their account and what happens to their data after deletion. It is provided for users and for Google Play account deletion verification.

---

## 1. How to Delete Your Account in the App

You can delete your account directly inside the Mkeka Leo mobile app by following these steps:

1. Open the **Mkeka Leo** mobile app.
2. Go to the **Account** section.
3. Tap your **name** on the account section.
4. Tap the **Delete Account** button.
5. Confirm that you want to delete your account.

After confirmation, the app will delete your account. Once your account is deleted, you may lose access to account-based features, saved preferences, and any VIP access connected to that account.

---

## 2. If You Need Help

If you need help before deleting your account, or if you cannot access your account, you can use the Help section inside the app:

1. Open the **Mkeka Leo** mobile app.
2. Tap the **Menu** icon in the top-right corner.
3. Open the **Help** section.
4. Follow the available support instructions.

You can also contact us by email at **[admin@mkekawaleo.com](mailto:admin@mkekawaleo.com)**.

---

## 3. What Data Is Deleted

When you delete your account, we delete or de-identify account information associated with your Mkeka Leo mobile app profile, including:

- Account profile information
- Contact details linked to the account
- App account identifiers
- Saved app preferences
- Account-based service activity
- VIP access records connected to the deleted account, where applicable

Deleting your account does not delete data held by third-party services under their own policies, such as payment providers, Google services, Meta platforms, or sports data providers.

---

## 4. Data We May Retain

Some information may be retained only when necessary for legal, security, fraud prevention, accounting, dispute resolution, or service integrity reasons.

Examples may include:

- Payment confirmation records
- Transaction references
- Fraud prevention logs
- Security logs
- Support messages you sent to us
- Records required by law or business compliance

Where retention is required, we keep only the minimum information needed for the required purpose.

---

## 5. Retention Period

Most account data is deleted or de-identified after account deletion is completed.

Some limited records may be retained for the following periods:

- Backup copies and technical logs may remain for up to **90 days** before being removed from normal backup cycles.
- Payment, accounting, fraud prevention, or legal compliance records may be retained for up to **5 years**, or longer if required by applicable law.
- Support messages may be retained for up to **24 months** to help us resolve disputes, safety issues, or service history questions.

After the applicable retention period ends, retained data is deleted or de-identified unless we are legally required to keep it longer.
